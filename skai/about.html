Drone Safety

The key to the passenger drone's operations is its automated flight system; its flight computer pulls data from onboard sensors and uses satellite navigation to chart the safest path. If damaged, it's designed to make a controlled descent to safe ground.

Meet the Fleet : 3 Drone Levels

First Class : Lilium Jet

Combining top speed with sleek design. Get to where you need to go before anyone else.

Second Class : Airbus

Flying with friends? No problem. The airbus can fly up to 4 passengers.

Third Class : Ehang

Know how to fly? Our single passenger drone. Completely safe, completely private.
